## LinkedIn / Portfolio Blurb

```text
Karpathy's Basement is a Discord multi-agent simulator for AI research education.

I built it to explore what happens when a chatbot becomes a room: multiple simulated research personas, separate Discord bot accounts, Gemini-based orchestration, persistent SQLite memory, contextual turn-taking, and a hidden director that keeps debates coherent.

The project taught me a lot about product feel in AI systems: timing, routing, memory, safety boundaries, and how much orchestration matters when you want agents to feel present instead of mechanical.
```
